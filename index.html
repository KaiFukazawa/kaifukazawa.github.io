Kai Fukazawa

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Mechanical Engineering at the University of California, Davis, advised by Iman Soltani at the Laboratory for AI, Robotics and Automation (LARA).

My research question is "how agents can learn better decision-making policies from past experience without making policy learning unnecessarily complex?" To address this, I focus on extracting useful structure from offline data and turning it into simple objectives for stable, safe, and scalable policy learning.

This perspective has led me to work across reinforcement learning and imitation learning for embodied agents, with applications such as robotics.
